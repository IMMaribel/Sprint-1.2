A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite websites. Open a new browser tab an see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Simple Bookmarking Speedy Searching Easy Sharing

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how manage your favorites sites.
